About Me

Where design meets development

I'm Ayoub Ghdiri — a creative developer and designer based in Tunisia. I specialize in crafting modern visuals and building functional web applications that deliver real value.

"I design with a developer’s mindset and build with a designer’s eye."

Currently a 2nd-year GLSI (Software Engineering & Information Systems) student at ISIMA, I combine technical skills in Python/Flask and front-end development with strong visual design expertise.

As the Webmaster of the IEEE ISIMA Student Branch, I lead the development of the branch’s digital presence — from building the official website to designing visual content and ensuring a consistent, modern brand identity across different activities and chapters.

From social media campaigns and branding to full web applications, I focus on creating clean, engaging, and user-centered experiences that are both aesthetic and practical.

Alongside my studies, I work on freelance projects and continuously push my skills through real-world challenges in both design and development.
